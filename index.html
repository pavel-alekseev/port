SONGLINE

Project task
Design user interface for music streaming app.

Product description
Songline is a music app that was created by independent musicians in search of a new way to grow a fan base and monetize their craft. Artists upload their music, get likes from listeners, and every week cash prizes are sent to the Top 50 artists.

Artists can mark selected content as Premium and choose a monthly fee for fans to pay in order to view that content. This allows artists to share material like unreleased songs, studio vlogs or secret meet and greet points with their fans, whilst monetizing the exclusive content.
