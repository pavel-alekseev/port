Project task
Design user interface for music streaming app.

Product description
Songline is an app that was created for independent musicians, it provides ways to grow a fan base and monetize original content. Artists upload their music and get votes from listeners, every week cash prizes are sent to the top artists. Artists can mark selected content as Premium and choose a monthly fee for fans to pay in order to view that content.

Mobile

Subscription process
I had to design subscription process, it should allow artists to mark content as premium and charge a monthly fee for access. The main issue was that the majority of apps artists don't have a big following to benefit from subscription fee.

To provide real value for that user group subscription system had to grow artists follower base, to make that possible I modified subscription functionality by adding "Follow to subscribe" option. As a monthly fee, it gives access to premium content but instead of money artists get new followers. The feature was implemented and is about to be tested on app release.
