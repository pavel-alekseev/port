Story

I am freelance designer with five years of experience in various fields. I studied graphic design for two years in Latvian design academy, after that worked full time as print designer for a few years and studied user interface design. Print design was a good foundation that allowed me to start working in other fields.

Now I mainly design web applications and work dirrectly with developer teams. I do print and branding projects from time to time to keep work diverse. As a freelancer, I can pick my own tools and design methods, it allows me to experiment with design process, I try to refine it and create my own workflow to produce design faster.
